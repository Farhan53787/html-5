Section I

Atoms

Atoms are the basic units of matter. They consist of a nucleus surrounded by electrons, with protons and neutrons in it.

Have you ever learned about different types of atoms?

Section II

Molecules

Molecules are made up of two or more atoms that are chemically bonded together. They can be simple, like oxygen (O2), or complex, like DNA. They can be either heterogeneous or homogeneous.

Did you drink water today? It contains molecules!

Section III

Ions

Ions are atoms that have gained or lost electrons, giving them an electric charge. They play a crucial role in many chemical reactions and biological processes. They may be positive (cations) or negative (anions).

Have you ever see plasma acting between wires? It is made up of ions!
